10 of my favorite TV shows/movies (unranked)

FYI the list isn't ordered

There are more but this is just 10 I like :)

Suits

It's just interesting to see how a genius selling weed got a job as a lawyer...

The drama that goes on in this lawyer show can be funny and sometimes interesting i guess

White House Down

This movie's about how the White House got infiltrated, and a veteran goes to save the president and a world war.

It's fun to see a veteran fight his way through the White House to save the president.

Shooter (2018)

An ex army sniper gets framed for shooting the president, but he manages to stay alive and away from the federal agents hunting for him.

It's just fun to see how he can't miss any sniper shot he takes.

Shooter (2007)

Shooter 2018 probably a "remake" of this movie, except it's split into seasons and episodes.

This movie is similar to the 2018 version. The sniper in this movie also manages to infiltrate a senator's house without getting killed, which is cool.

Designated Survivor

It's sad to see the Capitol blown in the first episode. A low level cabinet member was immediately sworn in as the President of the United States.

Throughout his presidential term, he faces many challenges and enemies, and even staff members who turn against him.

Olympus has Fallen

This probably won't happen in real life.

Some Korean officials manage to get past White House security and take control of the White House. However, it's interesting how an ex presidential detail agent manages to kill the Koreans mercenaries one by one and rescue the president.

The Final Table

Yes I also like watching cooking competitions.

It's fun to see how in a limited time, the chefs are able to create a dish with any ingredient they want.

It makes me kinda hungry too...

Hell's Kitchen

Yes watching Ramsay yell at chefs is funny-

There's always that one person in the kitchen that manages to mess up a dish...

"Where's the lamb sauce"

"It's RAWWWWWW"

6 Underground

6 ghosts (basically people who faked their death). Trying to save the world from something bad.

It's interesting to see how the operators create a huge magnetic device to disarm the guards on the ship.

Money Heist

It's just interesting to me how they spent months planning the whole process out, and it goes exactly as the professor planned.

It's like how the professor knows what the cops are going to do
